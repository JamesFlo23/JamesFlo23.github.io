Morals

I like to believe that my parents raised me to be a good person with good morals. Although I do believe in religion, and agree that it can be very helpful for those to find good morals, I was not raised religious, and therefore I did not find my morals through a god or higher power. I was taught at a very young age, what I like to believe is universally the most important characteristic in "good" people. And that is respect. Treat others how you would like to be treated. If everyone could adapt that characteristic, then I believe it could solve a lot of conflict and controversy we currently have in the world. I believe the solution to this problem is so valuable that you couldn't even put a number on it. And that is what I hope to achieve in my career/life.
